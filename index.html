Monetize Your Passion, Passively.

Generate revenue for your work without asking your fans to open their wallets.

Get Started

Fundraisr.me empowers creators to connect with their fans and generate revenue without requiring anyone to pay. We set you up with a ready-to-go webpage that mines for cryptocurrency on your behalf. The more fans that keep your webpage open while they surf the web, the more your make.

The Process

Passively monetize your content in just four steps.

Get a Monero Wallet

We pay you with Monero, a safe and secure cryptocurrency. In order to cash out, you’ll need a Monero Wallet. There are various Monero wallets out there, but the most popular is MyMonero.com.

Create a Fundraisr Account

Fill out the form below with some basic information. Within 24 hours, our Account Executives will create a personalized Fundraisr Page for you that is ready to mine for cryptocurrency.

Tell Your Fans

The goal is to get as many as your fans as possible to keep your Fundraisr Page open in their browsers. The more that keep it open, even if it’s in the background of what they are doing, the more cryptocurrency is mined for you.

Cash Out

You earn 65% of the Monero mined on your page. Once you meet the minimum threshold of a half of a coin, we’ll transfer the Monero right to your wallet where it is yours to keep!

About Monero

Monero is one of the largest cryptocurrencies in terms of market capitalization. Due to their focus on security and confidentiality, Monero is widely trusted by individuals and businesses alike to conduct transactions.

For more information, we recommend you visit Monero’s official site.
